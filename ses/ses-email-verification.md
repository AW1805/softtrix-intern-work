# Amazon SES Email Verification

Before sending emails using AWS services, I verified my email identity in Amazon SES.

### Steps

1. Open AWS Console
2. Go to Amazon SES
3. Navigate to **Verified Identities**
4. Click **Create identity**
5. Enter email address
6. AWS sends a verification email
7. Click the verification link in the email

### Result

The email identity was successfully verified and could now be used to send emails using Amazon SES.
